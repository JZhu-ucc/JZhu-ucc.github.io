Climate change is currently a serious problem, with scientists estimating that, at this rate, we could have large-scale extinction events by the end of this century caused by climate change. To combat this, one way that we can do so is by reducing the amount of goods we consume. As shown by the graph, we in north america are on the top of the carbon per capita graph. However, if we cut down on consumerism and reduce the amount of energy everyone uses, we can do our part to help stop climate change.
